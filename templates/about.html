About Apna Anaaj

Vickey Kumar Das

- Project Owner & MCA Student -

Namaste! My name is Vickey Kumar Das, and I am an MCA student who wants to help farmers through technology. Apna Anaaj is one such project that I have specially created for farmers like you. It is an effort to connect technology and agriculture to build a better future.

Our Mission & Vision

The purpose of this website is to empower farmers by providing them with accurate and timely information to make informed decisions related to farming. Our vision is to ensure every farmer receives the right knowledge, maximizing their efforts and increasing their profits.

What Apna Anaaj Offers
  • Real-time Price Prediction: Get daily updated market prices for various crops.
  • Sowing Recommendations: Receive personalized advice on crop sowing.
  • Image-based Crop Identification: Identify your crops by simply uploading a photo.
  • Community Forum: Connect with other farmers and share knowledge.
