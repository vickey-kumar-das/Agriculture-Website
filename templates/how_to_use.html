How to Use Apna Anaaj

Welcome to Apna Anaaj! This guide will help you understand how to use the various features of our application.

  • personUser Profile

    Your profile allows you to manage your personal information, including your location, soil type, preferred crops, and price alerts.

    Steps:

    1. Go to the "User Profile" section.
    2. Enter your location (City, State) and soil type.
    3. Add your preferred crops (comma-separated, e.g., Wheat, Rice, Maize).
    4. Set up price alerts for commodities (e.g., Wheat:2000,Rice:1500).
    5. Click "Update Profile" to save your changes.
  • local_floristSowing Recommendation

    Get personalized recommendations for sowing crops based on your location's weather and your soil type.

    Steps:

    1. Ensure your location and soil type are set in your User Profile.
    2. Go to the "Sowing Recommendation" section.
    3. Select a crop from the dropdown or upload an image of a crop.
    4. Click "Get Recommendation" to view detailed advice on optimal conditions, estimated profit/loss, fertilizer, and pest control.
  • show_chartCommodity Prices & Trends

    Explore current prices, forecast trends, and historical data for various commodities.

    Steps:

    1. From the homepage, click on any commodity (e.g., Wheat, Rice) under "Explore by commodity".
    2. View current prices, forecast trends, and historical WPI data presented in interactive charts.
  • forumCommunity Forum

    Connect with other farmers, ask questions, and share your experiences in the community forum.

    Steps:

    1. Go to the "Community Forum" section.
    2. Browse existing posts or click "Create New Post" to share your thoughts.
    3. Click on a post title to view its content and add comments.
